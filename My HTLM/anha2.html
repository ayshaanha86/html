My madrasa

an important Alia Madrasa in Khulna Division, Bangladesh . It is one of the most famous Alia Madrasas in Bangladesh at the divisional level. It was established in Khulna city in 1952. [ 1 ] [ 2 ] It is well-known for its Dakhil and Alim results. [ 3 ] It is used as a centre for Dakhil, Alim, Fazil, Kamil examinations of Khulna Division.

history of this My Madrasah.

This madrasa was established on 2 April 1952 in the heart of Khulna division. It was later approved by the Pakistan government on 1 July 1966. [ 1 ] Later, with the efforts of local people, this madrasa was gradually converted into Kamil Madrasa. In 2020, the then Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina inaugurated 50 models across the country, one of which is the Khulna Alia Madrasa Model Mosque . [ 4 ] [ 5 ] Local educationists in Khulna have long been demanding that this madrasa be converted into a government madrasa like the Dhaka Government Alia Madrasa and the Sylhet Government Alia Madrasa . [ 6 ] [ 7 ]

Educational activities

Khulna Alia Madrasa has primary education levels starting from Ebtedayi to the highest level of Alia Madrasa, Kamil. In addition, this madrasa has science and humanities departments at both Dakhil and Alim levels. Students of this madrasa participate in various competitions related to education. Students of the science department participate in science fairs. In addition, this madrasa has departments such as Al Quran and Islamic Studies, Al Hadith and Islamic Studies, Dawah, etc. at the Fazil and Kamil levels. Students of the Fazil and Kamil levels have the opportunity to do subject-based research. The institutional results of the madrasa are always at the top of the Khulna division. In addition, many students of this madrasa get admission in various universities in Bangladesh for higher education.

Facilities

This madrasa is located in the Khulna divisional city and is equipped with all modern facilities. The students have all kinds of facilities in this madrasa. There is a large playground, a library for studies, a research laboratory, a debating society for organizing debates. In addition, there is a common room for girls and sports facilities for spending time indoors.

Playground

The students of this madrasa have shown their sports literacy. Most of the time, the students play cricket, football and volleyball in the huge field of the madrasa. The students of the madrasa participate in various sports at the district and divisional levels. The madrasa authorities provide the development of the field and carry the sports equipment.

Model Mosque

Khulna Alia Madrasa has a modern mosque. This mosque was used by the students and teachers of the madrasa to offer prayers. In 2020, the then Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina inaugurated 50 models across the country, including the Alia Madrasa Model Mosque . One of these model mosques and Islamic cultural centers will be built near the Khulna Alia Kamil Madrasa on Khan Jahan Ali Road in Khulna. Both men and women can offer prayers in this four-story mosque.

Library

The madrasa has a library open to all students. All students from Dakhil to Kamil levels can borrow books from here and study. The madrasa library has many valuable books on subjects such as Al-Quran, Al-Hadith, Tafsir, Al-Fiqh, Islamic history, etc. The teachers and students of the madrasa can also come to this library and study.

Laboratory

A state-of-the-art science lab has been established in the madrasa for the students of the science department to conduct research and lab classes. Here, students can conduct research and experiments during teaching hours and in their free time.
